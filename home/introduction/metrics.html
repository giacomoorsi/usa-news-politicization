Metrics

In this data story we will refer several times to two main statistics that we computed: politiciazion and polarization. Let's define them.

Hey CNN, we want to know what the other party is doing well

Politicization

How often a news source reports quotes made by politicians.

Hey Fox News, we want to know the good news as well

Polarization

Whether a news source reports more often the others a particular politician or a political party.
